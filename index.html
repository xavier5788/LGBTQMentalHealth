Informed Consent

I am Xavier Bilon, studying Gender and Sexuality and Programming Applications in Electrical and Electronics Engineering at the University of the Philippines Diliman. I am doing a study on the mental health and well-being of LGBTQ+ students of the University of the Philippines Diliman in aid of legal policy and support advocacies within and outside the University.

This research will involve your participation in answering an on-line questionnaire that will take about 10-15 minutes.

Your participation in this research is entirely voluntary. It is your choice whether to participate or not. You may stop participating in the study at any time that you wish to.

I will not be sharing information about you to anyone. The information that I collect from this research project will be kept private. Any information about you will have an encrypted code on it as reference.

The questionnaire is in English.
